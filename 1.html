About Us — A Launchpad for Student Innovation

S2V at GVHSS Balussery is a trailblazing school-level innovation and entrepreneurship center. As part of the national STARS (Strengthening Teaching-Learning and Results for States) program, our hub equips students with future-ready skills — blending classroom learning with real-world application. Our aim is to nurture tech creators, not just tech consumers — giving students the confidence to build, test, and launch their own ventures.

🚀 Our Project — Smart Waste Management Using IoT

We are proud to lead a statewide initiative to support Kerala’s decentralized waste management mission.

  • 🛠️What We're Building: Student teams are designing and assembling IoT gateway devices that collect real-time data from Thumboormuzhi bins.
  • 📡Technology in Action: Devices monitor bin fill levels, usage patterns, and maintenance cycles — enabling smarter municipal waste collection and reporting.
  • 🤝Industry Collaboration: Partnership with Teamstarbase Pvt Ltd for technical mentorship and scale-ready expertise.
  • 🌍Statewide Impact: Pilots across multiple districts under Kerala’s Garbage-Free Cities 2.0 program.

Skills & Learning
